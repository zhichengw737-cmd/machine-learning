📘 How Does This Supervised Learning Lab Work?

🏷️

1. Labeled Guides

Supervised learning means the AI learns using an answer key! You act as the human teacher providing explicit guidance like "This is a Cat."

🔍

2. Feature Mapping

The AI doesn't look at the animal as a whole. Instead, it measures specific physical traits (like snout length or ear triangles) to map connections.

⚡

3. Statistical Weight

When you feed more examples, internal parameters strengthen. Facing unfamiliar shapes (like our Shiba), it makes mathematically weighted decisions!
